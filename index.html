We are a group of high school students from The Peddie School's Varsity Swim Team committed to making swimming accessible to all children. Through free lessons for kids ages 4-13, we're breaking down barriers and building water safety skills in our community.

DO I QUALIFY?

Before you sign up, make sure that your child:

Is 4 years or older

Is potty trained

Comes from a low income household according to the "2025 AFFORDABLE HOUSING REGIONAL INCOME LIMITS BY HOUSEHOLD SIZE"

(mostly) Speaks and understands English

Your name will not be shared
